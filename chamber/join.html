Membership Level

The Non-Profit membership option is for Non-Profit organizations. This membership provides the same benefits and features as the Gold Package.

The Bronze membership allows individuals and businesses one free use of the Chamber meeting hall per month and 20% off catering services.

The Silver membership allows individuals and businesses three free uses of the Chamber meeting hall per month, 20% off catering services, and 5 free coupon books for local shops and restaurants.

The Gold membership allows individuals and businesses five free uses of the Chamber meeting hall per month, 25% off catering services, and 10 free coupon books for local shops and restaurants.
